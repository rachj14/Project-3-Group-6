  • Mental Health Chart

    This Chart represents the mental health ranking for "Anxiety", "Depression", "Insomnia", "OCD" after listening to the various genres of music. This chart illustrates the mental health rankings associated with listening to various genres of music in relation to anxiety, depression, insomnia, and OCD. The rankings range from 0, indicating the least impact, to 10, representing the most adverse effects.

  • Anxiety (0-10): A ranking of 0 would mean no reported feelings of anxiety at all. Conversely, a ranking of 10 would indicate the highest level of anxiety, suggesting severe and constant feelings of worry, nervousness, or unease.
  • Depression (0-10): A ranking of 0 would signify no symptoms of depression, while a ranking of 10 would indicate severe and pervasive symptoms, such as profound sadness, hopelessness, or loss of interest in life.
  • Insomnia (0-10): A ranking of 0 would imply no difficulties with sleep whatsoever, whereas a ranking of 10 would suggest severe and persistent issues with falling asleep, staying asleep, or obtaining restful sleep.
  • OCD (Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder) (0-10): A ranking of 0 would suggest no symptoms of OCD, while a ranking of 10 would indicate the most severe symptoms, including intense and distressing obsessions and compulsions that significantly interfere with daily life.

    • In Conclusion, the 0-10 ranking scale provides a range from no reported symptoms (0) to the most severe symptoms (10) for each condition. Higher rankings indicate a greater impact and severity of the respective mental health condition on the individual's well-being and functioning.
